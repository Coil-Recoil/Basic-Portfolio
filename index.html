About Me

I'm a Software Developer-in-training currently attending University of Richmond USPC for Web Development. I've always loved working with and learning about new technology ever since I was a kid. I started with building and disassembling PC's and laptops as a hobby, and my interests have since grown to include building software technologies running on the silicon as well. My dream now is to combine my love of tech and thirst to solve challenges into a long software career.
